Shayari is a beautiful form of poetic expression that serves multiple purposes, deeply rooted in the cultural and literary traditions of South Asia.

It is a way to articulate emotions like love, longing, joy, and sorrow in a creative and rhythmic manner, often resonating with the reader or listener on a personal level.
